# SAMPLE

> Pedagogical branch holding worked examples that demonstrate every convention in real content. [[TEMPLATE]] is the blank skeleton; the rest ([[ACME WORKSPACE]], [[ATLAS SEARCH]], [[QUERY ROUTER]], [[MAYA CHEN]]) are a coherent fake product team showing how a real vault feels.

This whole branch is scaffolding. Read it once, then delete it with `rm -rf docs/sample/` when you're ready to write your own real content. The conventions all live in [[INFO]] — the samples just show them in action.

## What the examples demonstrate

* [[TEMPLATE]] — the blank skeleton you copy when creating a new doc. Shows every optional section.
* [[ACME WORKSPACE]] — an index/workspace doc. Demonstrates the `Parent of` taxonomy pattern.
* [[ATLAS SEARCH]] (child of ACME WORKSPACE) — a project doc. Demonstrates `Child of`, `Parent of`, and `Associated with` together.
* [[QUERY ROUTER]] (child of ATLAS SEARCH) — a sub-component doc. Demonstrates deep hierarchy.
* [[MAYA CHEN]] — a person doc. Demonstrates `Associated with` plus the prose-after-link convention, including inline `[[wiki-links]]` that are context hints rather than declared edges.
